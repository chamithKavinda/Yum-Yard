Classic Ramen Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
Chicken or vegetable broth 4 cups
Ramen noodles 2 packs
Soy sauce 2 tablespoons
Fresh ginger (grated) 1 tablespoon
Garlic (minced) 2 cloves
Carrots (julienned) 1/2 cup
Mushrooms (sliced) 1/2 cup
Green onions (chopped) 2 stalks
Baby spinach 1 cup
Soft-boiled eggs 2
Nori (seaweed) sheets 2
Sriracha or chili oil (optional) To taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Broth:

    In a large pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a simmer over medium heat. Add the soy sauce, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Cook the Noodles:

    While the broth is simmering, cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. Add Vegetables:

    Add the julienned carrots and sliced mushrooms to the broth. Let them cook for about 5 minutes until they are tender.

  4. Assemble the Ramen:

    Divide the cooked noodles into two bowls. Pour the hot broth and vegetables over the noodles. Top with chopped green onions, baby spinach, and halved soft-boiled eggs.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    Garnish each bowl with nori sheets and a drizzle of Sriracha or chili oil, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade ramen!
