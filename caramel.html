Caramel Recipe

Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity
Sugar 1 cup
Water 1/4 cup
Heavy Cream 1/2 cup
Unsalted Butter 2 tablespoons
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Instructions
Step Description
1 In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, combine the sugar and water. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
2 Once the sugar has dissolved, stop stirring and allow the mixture to come to a boil. Let it boil until it turns a deep amber color, about 10 minutes.
3 Remove the saucepan from the heat and carefully add the heavy cream. The mixture will bubble up significantly, so pour slowly and be cautious.
4 Stir in the butter until melted and combined.
5 Add the salt and vanilla extract, stirring to combine.
6 Allow the caramel to cool slightly before using. It will thicken as it cools.
