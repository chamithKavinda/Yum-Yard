Classic Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:

    In a large bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy. Add 2 cups of flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix until the dough begins to come together.

  2. Knead the Dough:

    Turn the dough out onto a floured surface. Knead the dough, gradually adding the remaining flour, until it is smooth and elastic, about 8-10 minutes. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.

  3. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.

  4. Shape the Dough:

    Punch down the dough to release any air bubbles. Divide the dough into two equal portions. On a floured surface, stretch and shape each portion into a round pizza crust.

  5. Add Toppings:

    Place the dough on a pizza peel or baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the crust. Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese and add your desired toppings.

  6. Bake the Pizza:

    Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or onto the pizza stone). Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  7. Serve:

    Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
