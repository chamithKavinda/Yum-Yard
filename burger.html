Delicious Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Patties

In a large bowl, mix the ground beef with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Form the mixture into 4 equal-sized patties.

Step 2: Cook the Patties

Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.

Step 3: Toast the Buns

While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.

Step 4: Assemble the Burgers

Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Place the lettuce leaves on top, followed by the burger patty with melted cheese, a slice of tomato, and a slice of onion. Cover with the top half of the bun.

Step 5: Serve

Serve the burgers hot with your favorite sides, such as fries or a salad. Enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!
